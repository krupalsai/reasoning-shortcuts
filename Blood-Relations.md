# 👨‍👩‍👧 Blood Relations — Shortcuts

## Key Relationships

| Relation | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Father's/Mother's son | Brother |
| Father's/Mother's daughter | Sister |
| Father's brother | Uncle |
| Father's sister | Aunt |
| Uncle's/Aunt's child | Cousin |
| Son's wife | Daughter-in-law |
| Wife's/Husband's brother | Brother-in-law |

## Shortcut: Draw a Family Tree!

Always sketch a tree when reading these problems.

```
       [Grandfather]
           |
      [Father] ── [Mother]
           |
    [You] ─── [Sibling]
```

## Gender Indicator Words

| Male clue words | Female clue words |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Son, Father, Uncle, Brother, Husband, He | Daughter, Mother, Aunt, Sister, Wife, She |

## Common Question Patterns

### Pattern 1: "A is B's ___"
- Draw A and B. Find relationship.

### Pattern 2: Coded relations
> "P $ Q" means P is father of Q
> "P @ Q" means P is mother of Q
Decode each symbol first, then solve.

### Pattern 3: "How is A related to B?"
- Trace: A → common person → B

## Practice

1. A is B's brother. B is C's father. C is D's sister. How is A related to D?
   → *A is D's uncle*

2. Pointing to a photo, Riya says "His mother is the only daughter of my mother."
   → *Riya is his mother → He is Riya's son*
